AboutUS

OUR MISSION
To Educate the Human Potential
Advance Skills Development Center (ASDC) is formed to help the students who could not continue their education with the required skills necessary for employment. ASDC will be affiliated with Department of Intermediate Education for the certification of these courses. The Department of Intermediate Education has introduced Short Term Vocational Certificate Courses with duration of 3 months, 6 months and 9 months to enable SSC failed and passed students who wish to join and earn their livelihood in a short span of time. The evaluation and certification of Short Term Vocational Certificate Courses are done by the State Institute of Vocational Education (SIVE) in the Commissionerate of Intermediate Education, Telangana State.
